How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly inside the Wallet Plan app. No need to contact support — the deletion is immediate and permanent.

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Open Wallet Plan on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom navigation bar).
  3. Scroll to the very bottom of the Settings screen.
  4. Tap "Delete Account".
  5. Read the warning message carefully.
  6. Enter your current password to confirm your identity.
  7. Tap "Delete" to permanently delete your account.

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently and immediately deleted:

Data Deleted
Your profile (name, email)✅ Yes
Your authentication credentials✅ Yes
All your personal financial accounts✅ Yes
All your transactions✅ Yes
Your app preferences and settings✅ Yes

If you are the family owner

If your account is the owner of a family group, deleting your account will also permanently delete:

  • The entire family group
  • All shared accounts belonging to the family
  • All transactions of all family members
  • All categories created within the family
  • All active invite links
⚠️ Other family members will lose access to all shared data. Their individual Wallet Plan accounts will remain active, but they will no longer have a family group.

If you are a family member (not the owner)

If you belong to a family but are not the owner, deleting your account will:

  • Remove you from the family group
  • Delete your personal profile and credentials
  • Leave the family and its shared data intact for other members

What Data Is Retained

After account deletion, no personal data is retained in our database.

The following may persist temporarily in third-party systems outside our direct control:

Service Data Retention
Sentry (crash reporting) Anonymized error logs linked to your user ID Up to 90 days, then auto-deleted
Google Firebase backups Encrypted database backups Up to 30 days, then auto-deleted

We do not retain financial data, transaction history, or personal identifiers beyond the deletion date.

Partial Data Deletion (without deleting your account)

You can delete specific data without deleting your account:

  • Delete individual transactions — tap any transaction and select Delete.
  • Delete financial accounts — go to Accounts, open an account, and select Delete Account. This also deletes all transactions associated with that account.
  • Leave a family group — go to Settings → Family → Leave Family. Your personal accounts and data remain intact.
  • Delete custom categories — go to Settings → Categories and delete any custom category you created.
