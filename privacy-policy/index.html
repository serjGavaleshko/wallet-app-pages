Privacy Policy — Wallet Plan

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Wallet Plan does not sell user data or use it for advertising purposes.

This Privacy Policy describes how Wallet Plan ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application available on iOS and Android.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Name — used to identify you within your family group
  • Email address — used for authentication, email verification, and password reset
  • Password — we do not have access to your password. Authentication is handled entirely by Firebase Authentication.

1.2 Financial Data

To provide the core functionality of the app, we store:

  • Account names, types (cash, card, savings), currencies, and balances
  • Transaction records: amounts, types (income, expense, transfer, debt), categories, descriptions, dates, and optional location notes
  • Savings goals
  • Debt records including debtor/creditor names you enter manually

1.3 Family & Collaboration Data

If you use family features:

  • Family name and membership information
  • Your role within the family (admin or member)
  • Your name and email are visible to other members of your family group
  • Invite codes you create or use

1.4 Usage & Diagnostic Data

We use Sentry for crash reporting and error monitoring. This may include:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App version
  • Error context and stack traces
  • Your user ID (to link errors to your account for support purposes)

Sentry may collect anonymous diagnostic data for crash analysis. No financial or personal content from your transactions is included in these reports.

We do not collect advertising identifiers or sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored using Google Firebase services (Google Cloud infrastructure).
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
  • Personal accounts can be marked as private — other family members cannot see them.
  • Sensitive local data (such as your PIN code) is stored using secure storage provided by the operating system.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We share data only with the following service providers, strictly to operate the app:

Note on currency rates: When fetching exchange rates, only a standard API request is made to retrieve publicly available rate data. No personal data, account information, financial transaction data, or financial details are sent to this service.

5. Family Data and Privacy

When you join a family group:

  • Your name and email are visible to other family members.
  • Shared accounts and their transactions are visible to all family members.
  • Personal (private) accounts are visible only to you, even within a family.
  • The family owner (admin) can remove members from the group.

When you leave or are removed from a family, you lose access to shared accounts and transactions. Your personal accounts remain accessible to you.

6. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Wallet Plan offers a Premium subscription that unlocks additional features. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment card details.

Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your App Store or Google Play account settings.

7. App Lock and Biometrics

If you enable the App Lock feature:

  • Your PIN code is stored locally on your device using the secure system storage provided by your operating system.
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint) is handled entirely by your device's operating system. Biometric data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

8. Children's Privacy

Wallet Plan is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Some data may be retained only if required for legal or security purposes.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

11. GDPR (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

We process your data to provide and improve app functionality and where required by law or your consent.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
